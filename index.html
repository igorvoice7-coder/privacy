Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Tatyana Bondar ("we", "us", or "our") built the Colori app (the "App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data that helps us improve the App. This data includes:

— Device type and operating system version
— App usage statistics (screens viewed, features used, session duration)
— Language and region settings
— Crash reports and performance data

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to personally identify you.

Subscriptions

The App offers optional premium subscriptions managed through Google Play. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information such as credit card numbers.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. The anonymous analytics data described above is collected regardless of age to improve the App experience.

Data Storage

Your coloring progress and saved pictures are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your artwork on any server. If you uninstall the App, your saved pictures will be deleted.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

— Google Play Services
— Firebase Analytics

Internet Connection

The App requires an internet connection to download additional content (coloring pictures) and to send anonymous analytics data. Downloaded content is cached on your device for offline use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
